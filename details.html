Inauguration of the Pleasuredome

Where

EasyJet and other airlines have regular flights to Thessaloniki and from there it's only 150km to Kalamitsi Campsite (by bus, hire car or hitching).

The campsite has its own beach, pool, bar and seaside bungalows. Check out their website.

When

On Sunday 14th September. Feel free to arrive a few days in advance and stay as long as you please. We will be there before and after the event. Not sure what exactly we'll be doing, but it will surely involve a lot of swimming and dancing.

What will happen

We don't know yet, but if you are there, it's going to be magical.

What you need to do

RSVP by email as soon as you can, and we will be able to block-book all the caravans on the beach, and establish a party domain. So let us know if you are in by the end of 2013, and we can put you in touch with the campsite to get your accommodation for as much time as you want.

Caravans accommodate four to six people, so you may want to think/book in groups.
